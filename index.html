PARTY-TIME

When we organize a party at home a big issue that we are facing is setting the right music. Usually we choose only one type of music for the entire house, so the big question is, will everyone be satisfied? A smart multi-room system comes to answer this question.

Find Out More

VISION

Our system will provide multiple party spaces, each playing a different style of music by interacting with the guests, so that there is a democratic approach to the music selection. Upon arrival, in order to ease the initial choice of the room, the guests are invited to select their preferred music taste so that the system can determine the profile of the user. After entering the correct room, a new voting system will be available among the guests as to what song they would like to be played next from a predefined, but large, playlist. After leaving the party, the guests will have complete access to the party's playlist which will be provided by the host, so that they can find the songs they enjoyed the most and listen to them again.

The last time you attended a party, did you wonder how it would be like if you were in charge of the music? With a smart Party-Time system you will be able to do it: your house will be transformed into a plethora of music parties. Also the undecided can be satisfied, enjoying a multitude of party at one!

AMI Features

intelligent
Auto adaptive room, every single room will understand the guest’s tastes, giving them the chance to listen their preferences
ubiquitus
It doesn’t matter in which room you are, the system is in that room and it’s independent from the others
responsive
The system respond in real time by taking the votes and make the playlist for the majority
adaptive
People going from a room to another bringing with them their preferences

System feature list

DEFCON 1
  1. The system recognizes the music taste of the guests.
  2. Each room has its lighting system and an independent audio system.
  3. The system will automatically update the playlist while the party is going.
  4. The next song is played by an interactive voting system.
  5. The lights color will identify a kind of music, for immediate feedback.
DEFCON 2
  1. The system will send the playlist to every guest after the party.
  2. The system starts with background music then after the expected number of people arrive at the party the playlist starts to play.
  3. if a guest choose a type of music that is not the same as the room where he is, the system can tell him in which room is playing the music he likes.
  4. Every guest is identified by a tag device, so every room will know the occupant location.
DEFCON 3
  1. An emergency interface can be implemented for safety reasons.
  2. Guests can interact to each other by seeing their profiles and their preferences.
  3. If in the playlist there are equal songs the the multi party house will merge and converge into a one single party ready to be splitted again.

HW and SW architecture

×
Hardware and use
See full list of components
Guests phone
After joining the wireless network, an iOs or Android device will be able to connect to the guest page of the system, choose the preferred kind of music.
Server
A raspberry-based server for syncing the rooms and storing the music file. Also demanded to store the guests preferences and interact with the PHILIPS HUE system. The information, such as votes and location, will be stored in a sqlite3 database. It's capable of sync with the client the Playlist throw wireless connection.
Client
Client based on raspberry pi or raspberry zero w, to receive ,from the server, the playlist already mixed for the guest preference and play throw the audio source.
Light system
Using PHILIPS HUE system, the server will assign a color for each kind of music available, and change them for every kind of music mutation. So the rooms are color-tagged, to provide visual feedback to users.
Audio system
Every audio capable system is compatible with the PartyTime ecosystem, using 3.5mm jack with no modification or GPIO interface to stream throw optic cable.
